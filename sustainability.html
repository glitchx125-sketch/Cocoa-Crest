Environmental Responsibility

Protecting the environment is part of how we do business. From sourcing to delivery, we look for ways to reduce our impact while keeping our chocolate exceptional.

Our goal is simple: enjoy premium chocolate today without compromising tomorrow.

Frequently Asked Questions

Where does your cocoa come from?

We source cocoa from partner farms across several growing regions, chosen for both bean quality and their commitment to sustainable practices.

Is your packaging recyclable?

Most of our packaging is already recyclable or low-plastic, and we're actively working toward full recyclability across every product line.

How do you support farming communities?

Through fair pricing, long-term partnerships and programs that help farmers invest in their land and communities for the long run.

What are your future sustainability goals?

We're working toward 100% recyclable packaging, expanding our reforestation efforts, and increasing the number of farming communities we directly support.
