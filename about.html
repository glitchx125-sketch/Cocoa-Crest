About Cocoa Crest

A modern cocoa and chocolate company built on quality and trust.

Our Story

Cocoa Crest is a modern cocoa and chocolate company dedicated to delivering premium cocoa-based products to customers and businesses. We carefully source high-quality cocoa beans and transform them into a diverse range of products.

From cocoa beans, powder and butter to dark chocolate, milk chocolate, gift collections and specialty ingredients, every product reflects our commitment to quality, innovation and customer satisfaction.

Our Mission

To craft premium cocoa and chocolate products while making them easy for everyone to explore and enjoy.

Our Vision

To become a trusted, product-focused chocolate brand known for quality, innovation and responsible sourcing.

Our Values

Quality, customer satisfaction, innovation and ethical, responsible sourcing in everything we do.

Our Journey

A few milestones along the way.

2018

Where It Started

Cocoa Crest began as a small-batch cocoa powder producer, working directly with a handful of local farms.

2020

First Chocolate Bars

We expanded into dark and milk chocolate bars, refining our recipes with feedback from early customers.

2022

Truffles & Gifting Line

Launched our truffles and gift collections, making Cocoa Crest a go-to choice for celebrations and corporate gifting.

2024

Nationwide Shipping

Grew our supply chain to deliver fresh cocoa and chocolate products across the country.

2026

Today

A growing catalogue of beans, powders, bars, truffles and gifts — still rooted in the same care we started with.

From Bean to Bar

How a cocoa pod becomes the chocolate you enjoy.

1

Harvest

Cocoa pods are hand-picked at peak ripeness by our partner farms.

2

Ferment & Dry

Beans are fermented and sun-dried to develop their rich flavour.

3

Roast & Grind

Beans are roasted and ground into cocoa mass, butter and powder.

4

Craft & Package

We blend, mould and pack every product with care before it ships to you.

Quality You Can Trust

We work closely with farmers and partners to ensure every bean meets our high standards. The result is chocolate that is consistent, flavourful and made with care.

  • Carefully selected, high-quality cocoa beans
  • Modern, hygienic production methods
  • Products for both individuals and businesses
  • A growing range of flavours and gift options
